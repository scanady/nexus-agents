# AdSense Review Criteria — Complete Scored Checklist

This is the complete set of criteria used to evaluate a site for Google AdSense approval. Each criterion maps to official Google documentation.

---

## Category 1: Content Quality & Depth (Weight: HIGHEST)

This is the single most important category. "Low value content" is the #1 rejection reason.

### 1.1 Sufficient Content Volume
- **Minimum pages:** Site should have at least 15-30 pages with substantive content
- **Per-page minimum:** Each content page should have 300-500+ words of original text
- **Complete content:** All pages must be fully built — no "coming soon" or placeholders
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: 30+ pages of rich (600+ word) content
  - 8: 20-30 pages with adequate (300-600 word) content
  - 6: 15-20 pages with adequate content
  - 4: 10-15 pages, some thin
  - 2: Fewer than 10 content pages
  - 0: Fewer than 5 pages or mostly thin/empty content

### 1.2 Content Originality
- Content must be original — not copied, scraped, or syndicated from other sites
- Must not be auto-generated or AI-generated without substantial human editorial input
- Must not be spun/rewritten versions of existing content
- Must add unique value beyond what's available on similar sites
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Clearly original, expert-level content throughout
  - 8: Mostly original with clear author voice
  - 6: Original but generic — doesn't stand out from competitors
  - 4: Some original content mixed with templated/generic material
  - 2: Mostly generic, templated, or appears AI-generated without editing
  - 0: Scraped, copied, or auto-generated content

### 1.3 Content Depth & Value
- Does each page provide substantial, useful information?
- Would a user find genuine value from visiting?
- Is there enough depth that a user would spend time reading?
- Does the content answer questions, solve problems, or provide insights?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Comprehensive, expert content that thoroughly covers topics
  - 8: Good depth — informative and useful
  - 6: Adequate depth — covers topics at a basic level
  - 4: Shallow — touches on topics without real depth
  - 2: Very thin — mostly surface-level or filler content
  - 0: No meaningful content value

### 1.4 Content Focus & Niche
- Does the site have a clear topic, niche, or theme?
- Is the content cohesive (related topics) vs. random?
- Can Google determine what the site is "about"?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Clear, well-defined niche with consistent topic coverage
  - 8: Clear general topic with good coverage
  - 6: Identifiable topic but somewhat scattered
  - 4: Vague theme or very broad without depth
  - 2: No clear topic — random collection of pages
  - 0: Cannot determine what the site is about

### 1.5 Content Freshness
- Is content dated and regularly updated?
- Are there recent posts/updates?
- Does the site appear actively maintained?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Regular updates (weekly or more), recent content visible
  - 8: Regular updates (monthly), content within last few months
  - 6: Some recent content, sporadic updates
  - 4: Content appears dated, last update months ago
  - 2: Content appears stale, possibly abandoned
  - 0: No dates visible, appears unmaintained

### 1.6 Text-to-Visual Ratio
- Is there enough text content relative to images/video/other media?
- Sites that are primarily images, video, or Flash without substantial text will fail
- Text must be in complete sentences and paragraphs, not just headlines/captions
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Rich text content supplemented by relevant visuals
  - 8: Good text content with supporting visuals
  - 6: Adequate text but could use more
  - 4: Light on text, heavy on visuals
  - 2: Mostly images/video with minimal text
  - 0: No substantive text — only visuals or captions

## Category 2: User Experience & Navigation (Weight: HIGH)

### 2.1 Navigation Clarity
- Is the navigation bar/menu present and functional?
- Can users understand the site structure immediately?
- Are navigation labels clear and descriptive?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Excellent navigation — clear, intuitive, comprehensive
  - 7: Good navigation — functional and reasonably organized
  - 4: Basic navigation — exists but could be clearer
  - 2: Poor navigation — confusing or incomplete
  - 0: No navigation or completely broken

### 2.2 Site Organization
- Is content organized logically (categories, tags, sections)?
- Can users find specific content easily?
- Is there a logical hierarchy?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Excellent organization with categories, breadcrumbs, search
  - 7: Good organization — content is logically grouped
  - 4: Basic organization — some structure visible
  - 2: Poor organization — content is scattered
  - 0: No discernible organization

### 2.3 Mobile Responsiveness
- Does the site render properly on mobile devices?
- Is text readable without zooming?
- Are buttons/links tapable on mobile?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Fully responsive, excellent mobile experience
  - 7: Responsive, minor issues
  - 4: Partially responsive — some elements break
  - 2: Poor mobile experience
  - 0: Not mobile-friendly at all

### 2.4 Page Load Performance
- Do pages load within a reasonable time?
- Are there large blocking resources?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Fast loading (< 2 seconds)
  - 7: Acceptable loading (2-4 seconds)
  - 4: Slow loading (4-6 seconds)
  - 2: Very slow (6+ seconds)
  - 0: Pages fail to load or time out

### 2.5 No Broken Links
- Do all internal links work?
- Are there 404 errors?
- Do external links work?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: No broken links found
  - 7: 1-2 minor broken links
  - 4: Several broken links
  - 2: Many broken links affecting navigation
  - 0: Critical navigation links broken

### 2.6 No Intrusive Elements
- No excessive pop-ups or modals
- No auto-playing video with sound
- No deceptive download buttons or confusing interstitials
- Better Ads Standards compliance
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Clean, user-friendly experience
  - 7: Minor interstitials (cookie consent is OK)
  - 4: Some intrusive elements
  - 2: Multiple intrusive elements
  - 0: Abusive ad experiences or severely intrusive elements

## Category 3: Site Identity & Trust (Weight: MEDIUM-HIGH)

### 3.1 About Page
- Does an About page exist?
- Does it explain who runs the site, the mission, or the publisher?
- Does it establish credibility/expertise?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Detailed About page with real identity, expertise, mission
  - 7: About page with adequate information
  - 4: Minimal About page
  - 2: About page exists but is nearly empty
  - 0: No About page

### 3.2 Contact Information
- Is there a Contact page or method?
- Does it provide a real way to reach the publisher?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Contact page with form, email, and/or physical address
  - 7: Contact page with form or email
  - 4: Minimal contact info (just email)
  - 2: Contact link exists but doesn't work or is minimal
  - 0: No contact information

### 3.3 Professional Appearance
- Does the site look trustworthy and professional?
- Is the design clean and consistent?
- Are there obvious template artifacts or unfinished elements?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Professional, polished design
  - 7: Clean design, looks credible
  - 4: Acceptable but clearly template-based
  - 2: Looks unfinished or unprofessional
  - 0: Spam-like or extremely low quality appearance

### 3.4 Author Attribution
- Is content attributed to an author?
- Is there author expertise/credentials?
- (Contributes to E-E-A-T signals)
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Clear authorship with credentials/bio
  - 7: Author names visible on content
  - 4: Some attribution
  - 2: Minimal or no attribution
  - 0: Anonymous, no publisher identity

## Category 4: Technical Compliance (Weight: MEDIUM)

### 4.1 HTTPS
- Is the site served over HTTPS?
- Is the SSL certificate valid?
- **Score:** PASS (10) or FAIL (0)

### 4.2 ads.txt
- Does `/ads.txt` exist?
- Is it properly formatted?
- Does it include the correct Google publisher ID?
- Returns HTTP 200 with text/plain content type?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Valid ads.txt with correct Google entry
  - 5: ads.txt exists but has issues
  - 0: No ads.txt or inaccessible

### 4.3 Privacy Policy
- Does a privacy policy page exist?
- Is it linked from the main navigation or footer?
- Does it disclose:
  - Use of cookies?
  - Third-party ad serving (Google)?
  - How data is collected and used?
  - Opt-out information?
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Complete privacy policy with all required disclosures
  - 7: Privacy policy exists with most disclosures
  - 4: Privacy policy exists but missing key disclosures
  - 2: Privacy policy exists but is inadequate
  - 0: No privacy policy

### 4.4 Terms of Service
- Does a Terms of Service page exist?
- **Score:** 10 (exists and complete), 5 (exists but thin), 0 (missing)

### 4.5 Sitemap
- Does `/sitemap.xml` exist?
- Is it properly formatted?
- Does it list all important pages?
- **Score:** 10 (valid sitemap), 5 (exists but issues), 0 (missing)

### 4.6 Robots.txt
- Does `/robots.txt` exist?
- Is it blocking important content from crawlers?
- Does it allow Googlebot access?
- **Score:** 10 (proper robots.txt), 5 (exists, minor issues), 0 (blocking content)

---

## Category 5: Policy Compliance (Weight: MEDIUM)

### 5.1 No Prohibited Content
- Site does not contain content from any prohibited category (illegal, adult, violent, deceptive, etc.)
- **Score:** PASS (10) or FAIL (0)

### 5.2 Ad Placement (if ads already present)
- Ads don't appear on error pages, empty pages, or under-construction pages
- Ads don't interfere with navigation
- Content volume exceeds ad volume
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: No ad placement issues (or no ads yet)
  - 5: Minor issues
  - 0: Significant violations

### 5.3 Supported Language
- Content is primarily in a language supported by AdSense
- **Score:** PASS (10) or FAIL (0)

### 5.4 No Deceptive Content
- No misleading claims, fake downloads, or deceptive UI
- No cloaking or doorway pages
- **Score:** PASS (10) or FAIL (0)

---

## Category 6: Search Engine Readiness (Weight: MEDIUM-LOW)

### 6.1 SEO Fundamentals
- Pages have proper title tags
- Pages have meta descriptions
- Content uses proper heading hierarchy (H1, H2, H3)
- Images have alt text
- **Scoring:**
  - 10: Excellent SEO fundamentals throughout
  - 7: Good — most pages have proper tags
  - 4: Basic — some pages missing tags
  - 2: Poor — most pages have SEO issues
  - 0: No SEO fundamentals

### 6.2 Indexability
- Pages are not blocked from indexing (no noindex on content pages)
- Site is likely indexed by Google
- **Score:** PASS (10) or FAIL (0)

## Scoring Summary

| Category | Max Score | Weight |
|----------|-----------|--------|
| Content Quality & Depth (6 criteria × 10) | 60 | HIGHEST |
| User Experience & Navigation (6 criteria × 10) | 60 | HIGH |
| Site Identity & Trust (4 criteria × 10) | 40 | MEDIUM-HIGH |
| Technical Compliance (6 criteria × 10) | 60 | MEDIUM |
| Policy Compliance (4 criteria × 10) | 40 | MEDIUM |
| Search Engine Readiness (2 criteria × 10) | 20 | MEDIUM-LOW |

### Verdict Thresholds (Normalized to Category Scores)

**APPROVE (Likely to Pass):**
- Content Quality: No criterion below 6/10 AND average ≥ 7/10
- All other categories: No FAIL scores, average ≥ 6/10
- No critical issues

**LIKELY REJECT (Borderline):**
- Content Quality: 1-2 criteria at 4-5/10 OR average 5-6/10
- Other categories mostly passing but with some warnings

**REJECT (Will Not Pass):**
- Content Quality: Any criterion at 0-3/10 OR average below 5/10
- OR any critical technical/policy failure
- OR any prohibited content found
