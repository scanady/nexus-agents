# Privacy Policy Requirements for AdSense

Publishers must have a privacy policy that meets Google's requirements. This reference covers required disclosures and provides a template section for AdSense compliance.

## Required Disclosures

Your privacy policy MUST disclose:

### 1. Third-Party Advertising
- Third parties (including Google) use cookies to serve ads
- Cookies enable serving ads based on user visits to your site and other sites
- Users may opt out of personalized advertising

### 2. Data Collection Technologies
- Use of cookies
- Web beacons / pixels
- IP address collection
- Other identifiers used

### 3. Data Sharing
- That data may be shared with advertising partners
- How Google uses data when you use partner sites

### 4. User Choices
- How users can opt out of personalized ads
- Link to Google's ad settings or NAI opt-out

## Required Link (Option)

Google provides an approved disclosure page you can link to:

```
https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/
```

Title: "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps"

## Privacy Policy Template Section

Add this section to your privacy policy for AdSense compliance:

---

### Advertising

**Third-Party Advertising**

We use third-party advertising companies, including Google AdSense, to serve ads when you visit our website. These companies may use information about your visits to this and other websites in order to provide advertisements about goods and services of interest to you.

**Cookies and Web Beacons**

Third-party vendors, including Google, use cookies to serve ads based on your prior visits to this website or other websites. Google's use of advertising cookies enables it and its partners to serve ads to you based on your visit to this site and/or other sites on the Internet.

**Opting Out of Personalized Advertising**

You may opt out of personalized advertising by visiting [Google Ads Settings](https://www.google.com/settings/ads). Alternatively, you can opt out of third-party vendor's use of cookies for personalized advertising by visiting [www.aboutads.info](http://www.aboutads.info/choices/).

**How Google Uses Data**

To learn more about how Google collects and uses data when you use our website, please visit: [How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps](https://www.google.com/policies/privacy/partners/).

---

## Additional Requirements by Region

### EU/EEA/UK (GDPR)
- Obtain consent before setting non-essential cookies
- Cookie consent banner required
- Right to access, correct, delete personal data
- Data processing lawful basis disclosure

### California (CCPA)
- "Do Not Sell My Personal Information" link (if applicable)
- Disclosure of categories of personal information collected
- Right to know and delete personal information

### COPPA (US - Children)
- If site is directed at children under 13:
  - Parental consent requirements
  - Cannot use personalized advertising
  - Must tag site/requests appropriately

## Placement Requirements

- Privacy policy must be **easily accessible**
- Link in website footer (recommended)
- Link before data collection points (forms, etc.)
- Available on all platforms (web, mobile)

## Privacy Policy Checklist

Before monetizing with AdSense, verify your privacy policy:

- [ ] Discloses use of cookies for advertising
- [ ] Mentions third-party ad serving (Google)
- [ ] Explains how user data may be used for ad personalization
- [ ] Provides opt-out information
- [ ] Links to Google's privacy information (optional but recommended)
- [ ] Is easily accessible from all pages
- [ ] Covers all data collection on your site
- [ ] Updated date is recent
- [ ] Matches your actual data practices

## Common Privacy Policy Issues

| Issue | Problem | Fix |
|-------|---------|-----|
| Missing policy | No privacy policy at all | Create one immediately |
| No ad disclosure | Doesn't mention advertising | Add advertising section |
| Outdated | Hasn't been updated | Review and update |
| Not accessible | Link broken or hidden | Fix link, add to footer |
| Incomplete | Missing required disclosures | Add missing sections |
| Wrong practices | Policy doesn't match reality | Align policy with practices |

## Policy Review Frequency

Review privacy policy when:
- Adding new advertising partners
- Changing data collection practices
- Laws/regulations change
- Google updates requirements
- At minimum: annually
