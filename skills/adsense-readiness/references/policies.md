# Google Publisher Policies Reference

Complete reference for AdSense program policies. Publishers must comply with all policies to monetize content with Google ads.

## Content Policies

### Prohibited Content Categories

**Illegal Content**
- Content that is illegal, promotes illegal activity, or infringes legal rights

**Intellectual Property Abuse**
- Copyright infringement
- Counterfeit product sales or promotion

**Dangerous or Derogatory Content**
- Hate speech targeting protected characteristics
- Harassment, bullying, or intimidation
- Threats or advocacy of harm
- Content supporting terrorist organizations
- Extortion or exploitation

**Animal Cruelty**
- Promotion of animal cruelty for entertainment
- Sale of products from endangered species

**Misrepresentative Content**
- Misleading representation of publisher, content, or purpose
- False affiliation or endorsement claims
- Unreliable health claims contradicting scientific consensus
- Climate change denial
- Deceptive practices (phishing, "get rich quick" schemes)
- Manipulated media on political/social issues

**Enabling Dishonest Behavior**
- Fake documents, term papers, exam-taking services
- Hacking/cracking tools and instructions
- Unauthorized tracking/surveillance tools

**Sexually Explicit Content**
- Graphic sexual content in any format
- Non-consensual sexual themes
- Deepfake pornography creation or distribution

**Compensated Sexual Acts**
- Prostitution, escort services, "sugar" dating

**Mail Order Brides**
- International marriage broker services

**Adult Themes in Family Content**
- Content appearing family-friendly but containing adult themes

**Child Sexual Abuse and Exploitation**
- Any content exploiting or endangering children

## Behavioral Policies

### Dishonest Declarations
Information provided to Google must be:
- Materially accurate and complete
- Not misleading or deceptive
- Includes accurate ads.txt/app-ads.txt entries
- Accurate URLs and location data in ad requests

### Ads Interfering

**Prohibited ad placements:**
- Overlaying or adjacent to navigation items causing accidental clicks
- Severely interfering with content consumption
- Covering content or pushing it off display
- "Dead end" screens where user must click ad to exit

### Inventory Value

**No ads on screens:**
- Without publisher content or with low-value content
- Under construction
- Used for alerts, navigation, or behavioral purposes only

**Low-value content definition:** Unintelligible content with no user value (e.g., filler text, "lorem ipsum")

**Out of Context Ads - Prohibited:**
- Apps/pages running in background
- Ads appearing outside display area
- When user attention is expected elsewhere
- Unclear association between ad and publisher content

**Replicated Content:**
- No ads on pages with embedded/copied content without added value
- Must comply with intellectual property policies

**Ad-to-Content Ratio:**
- More publisher content than ads/promotional material required

**Language Requirements:**
- Content must be primarily in a supported language

## Privacy-Related Policies

### Personalized Advertising Restrictions

**Cannot use for personalized targeting:**
- Users known to be under 13
- Activity on child-directed sites/apps
- Activity on adult, gambling, or government sites
- Sensitive information including:
  - Health/medical information
  - Financial status details
  - Racial/ethnic origins
  - Religious beliefs
  - Criminal records
  - Political affiliation
  - Trade union membership
  - Sexual orientation

**US/Canada Housing, Employment, Credit Restrictions:**
Cannot target based on gender, age, parental status, marital status, or ZIP code for:
- Housing listings and real estate
- Employment and job ads
- Credit, banking, financial services

### Privacy Disclosures

**Required in privacy policy:**
- Disclosure of data collection, sharing, and usage
- Mention of cookies, web beacons, IP addresses, identifiers
- Disclosure that third parties place/read cookies
- Option: Link to "How Google uses data when you use our partners' sites or apps"

### Technical Privacy Requirements

- Cannot set cookies on Google domains
- Cannot pass personally identifiable information to Google
- Cannot merge PII with non-PII without opt-in consent
- Must comply with EU user consent policy
- Must disclose and get consent for precise location data collection

### COPPA Compliance

For sites/apps covered by COPPA:
- Notify Google via Search Console or tag requests appropriately
- No interest-based advertising for users under 13
- No remarketing on child-directed content

## Requirements and Other Standards

### Google Search Spam Policies
- No ads on pages violating Google Search spam policies

### Abusive Experiences
- No ads on pages with abusive experiences

### Malware and Unwanted Software
- No malware, viruses, ransomware, etc.
- Must comply with Google's Unwanted Software policy

### Better Ads Standards
- Must conform to Coalition for Better Ads standards
- See betterads.org for disallowed ad experiences

### Authorized Inventory (ads.txt)
- If using ads.txt, must be listed as authorized seller
- Parents must ensure Children add ads.txt with Parents as authorized sellers

### Sanctions Compliance

**Unavailable regions:**
- Crimea
- Cuba
- DNR and LNR
- Iran
- North Korea

Cannot serve restricted entities or individuals under trade sanctions.

## Policy Enforcement

Violations may result in:
- Ads blocked from appearing on content
- Account suspension
- Account termination

After fixing violations, you can request a site review.
